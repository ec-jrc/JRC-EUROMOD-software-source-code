Distributional Tables

2.6. Mean equivalised disposable income

Description

The Table shows the annual mean of equivalised disposable income on individual level, using weights.
Disposable income is defined by the income list ils_dispy and equivalised using the modified OECD equivalence scale.
The mean is shown for the whole population, as well as broken down over a breakdown variable (e.g. income deciles, see Breakdown).
Results can be restricted to a specific population group (see Target population).

Arguments
  • Breakdown:
    Default: deciles of individual equivalised disposable income
    For details with respect to definition see Distributional Tables / General argmuments / Breakdown
  • Level of analysis:
    Individual (can not be changed)
  • Target population:
    Default: empty, i.e. whole population
    For details with respect to definition see Distributional Tables / General argmuments / Target population
