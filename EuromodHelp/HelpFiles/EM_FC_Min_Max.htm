The policy functions Min and Max

The functions Min and Max calculate the maximum respectively the minimum of a number of values. In (the not very realistic) example below the minimum of employment income (yem), "some income" (defined by the incomelist il_someinc) and a monthly amount of 100 is calculated.

Example1:
Policy SL_demo Comment
Min on
val yem
val il_someinc
val 100#m
output_var stm01_s
TAX_UNIT individual_sl

Please note, that formula parameters provide the operators MIN(2) and MAX(2), which may allow a more efficient implementation of minima and maxima. e.g. min(10, 15), max(3,8), etc.
